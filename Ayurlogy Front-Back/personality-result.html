🕊️ Your Daily Routine

Follow this routine for your dosha. The table below shows phases, time, focus, and activities.

🕊️ DAILY ROUTINE – VATA DOSHA (Grounding · Calm · Stability)

PhaseTimeFocusActivities
🌅 Morning 6:00 – 9:00 AM Calm mind + digestion activation
  • Wake up same time daily (very important for Vata)
  • Warm water (optional ginger infusion)
  • Gentle stretching / slow yoga (10–15 min)
  • Breathing: deep nasal breathing or box breathing (5 min)
  • Light self-reflection or journaling (mental grounding)
🧠 Mental health: reduces anxiety, restlessness · 🔥 Digestion: warms digestive fire gently
💼 Daytime 9:00 AM – 1:00 PM Steady productivity
  • Work/study in 90-minute focused blocks
  • Avoid multitasking
  • Mid-morning snack if needed (warm, simple food)
  • Lunch by 12:30 PM (largest meal)
🧠 Prevents overwhelm · 🔥 Strongest digestion window
🌤️ Afternoon 1:00 – 6:00 PM Prevent fatigue
  • Short walk after lunch
  • Light work only
  • Avoid excess caffeine
  • 5-minute grounding pause (feet on floor, deep breathing)
🌙 Evening 6:00 – 10:00 PM Wind down nervous system
  • Early, light dinner (before 7:30 PM)
  • Digital detox after 9 PM
  • Warm shower
  • Gratitude or calming music
  • Sleep by 10 PM
🧠 Improves sleep quality · 🔥 Prevents bloating & gas

🔥 DAILY ROUTINE – PITTA DOSHA (Balance · Cooling · Emotional ease)

PhaseTimeFocusActivities
🌅 Morning 5:30 – 8:30 AM Calm intensity
  • Wake up without rushing
  • Room-temperature water
  • Cooling stretches / yoga (no competition mindset)
  • Breathing: slow exhale-focused breathing
  • Short mindfulness or intention setting
🧠 Reduces irritability · 🔥 Avoids overheating digestion
💼 Daytime 9:00 AM – 1:00 PM Productive but not obsessive
  • Deep work in structured blocks
  • Short breaks every hour
  • Lunch at peak hunger (12–1 PM)
🧠 Prevents burnout · 🔥 Supports strong digestion without acidity
🌤️ Afternoon 1:00 – 6:00 PM Emotional regulation
  • Avoid skipping meals
  • Walk in nature if possible
  • Cooling beverages (not iced)
  • Step away from conflict/stress if noticed
🌙 Evening 6:30 – 10:30 PM Release control
  • Light dinner
  • Reflection without self-criticism
  • Screen brightness low
  • Gentle reading or music
  • Sleep by 10:30 PM
🧠 Calms anger/frustration · 🔥 Prevents heartburn & acid reflux

PhaseTimeFocusActivities
🌅 Morning 5:00 – 7:30 AM Energy & momentum
  • Wake up early (before heaviness sets in)
  • Warm water
  • Dynamic movement (walk, yoga, light cardio)

  • Avoid naps
  • Engage socially or creatively
🌙 Evening 6:00 – 10:00 PM Lightness + clarity
  • Very light dinner (or soup)
  • Short walk
  • Reflection on progress
  • Sleep by 10 PM
🧠 Lifts emotional heaviness · 🔥 Prevents sluggish metabolism
