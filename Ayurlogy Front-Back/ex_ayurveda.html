The Three Doshas

Vata — Movement & Creativity

Elements: Air + Space

  • Slim build, dry skin, cold hands and feet
  • Quick thinking, creative, energetic bursts
  • Imbalance: anxiety, insomnia, gas, constipation

Tip: Prefer warm, cooked meals, regular routines, gentle yoga and oil massage.

Pitta — Transformation & Clarity

Elements: Fire + Water

  • Medium build, strong digestion, sharp intellect
  • Imbalance: anger, acidity, inflammation

Tip: Favor cooling foods, avoid excess spice, and include calming practices.

Kapha — Structure & Stability

Elements: Earth + Water

  • Solid build, smooth skin, steady energy
  • Imbalance: weight gain, sluggish digestion, lethargy

Tip: Stay active daily, eat light and warm foods, avoid excess sweets.

Mixed Dosha Types

Most people are dual types (e.g., Vata-Pitta). Balance both tendencies by choosing habits that calm the currently aggravated dosha.

Agni, Ama & Digestion

Ayurveda teaches that health begins with digestion. Strong Agni supports energy, immunity, and mental clarity.

Agni

The digestive fire responsible for digestion and metabolism.

Ama

Undigested residue that accumulates when digestion is weak.

Simple habits like regular meals, warm food, and mindful eating gently strengthen Agni and prevent imbalance.

Safety & Practical Notes

  • Ayurveda complements modern medicine.
  • Do not stop prescribed medication without medical advice.
  • Use reputable herbal sources.

Final Thought

Ayurveda’s power lies in personalization. Small daily choices — what you eat, when you sleep, how you move — create lasting balance over time.
