Know Your Mental state

Ayurveda sees the mind as a powerful bridge between the body, senses, and soul. Understanding your mental nature is the first step toward emotional balance and clarity.

In Ayurveda, the mind (Manas) is considered an inner sense organ. It processes thoughts, emotions, awareness, and sensory experiences. Your mental constitution, called Manas Prakriti, is shaped by three fundamental qualities known as Gunas.

Sattva

Represents clarity, harmony, calmness, and wisdom. A Sattvic mind is peaceful, focused, emotionally stable, and inclined toward truth.

Rajas

Represents activity, ambition, desire, and restlessness. Excess Rajas can lead to anxiety, stress, impatience, and overthinking.

Tamas

Represents inertia, dullness, heaviness, and ignorance. When dominant, it may cause low motivation, confusion, laziness, or sadness.

How to Calm and Strengthen the Mind

  • Practice daily meditation or mindful breathing
  • Maintain regular sleep and waking times
  • Use gentle self-care practices like oil massage and calming music
  • Consume fresh, light, and nourishing foods
